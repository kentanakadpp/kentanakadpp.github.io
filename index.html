Welcome to Ken'ichiro Tanaka's home page. I am an associate professor of the University of Tokyo.

Research

My research interest is in numerical analysis and computational methods in science and engineering.

  • Function approximation, numerical integration, and their application to equations
    • Double-exponential (DE) Sinc methods
    • Kernel quadrature
  • Mathematical optimization
  • Numerical methods for economics and finance
  • Numerical computation with guaranteed accuracy

Please see my research and publication list for more details.
