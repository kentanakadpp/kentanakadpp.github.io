News 2015-2019

[Dec.17, 2019] A new preprint "Kernel-based interpolation at approximate Fekete points", written in collaboration with Dr. Toni Karvonen and Prof. Simo Sarkka has been released (arXiv:1912.07316).

[Sep. 17, 2019] I gave a talk entitled "Estimate of the best approximation of analytic functions and construction of approximation formulas for them by mathematical optimization" at the MSJ Autumn Meeting 2019 at Kanazawa University. This is a talk invited by the Applied Mathematics Research Section of the MSJ and concerned with my recent research about constructing approximation formulas via mathematical optimization.

[Aug.29, 2019] The paper entitled "Generation of collocation points in the method of fundamental solutions for 2D Laplace's equation" has been published in JSIAM Letters. This paper is written in collaboration with Hiroaki Hirano, my former student in master's course.

[Aug.16, 2019] My paper entitled "Generation of point sets by convex optimization for interpolation in reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces" has been published in Numerical Algorithms.

[Jun.10, 2019] A new preprint "Convergence analysis of approximation formulas for analytic functions via duality for potential energy minimization", written in collaboration with Satoshi Hayakawa, has been released (arXiv:1906.03133).

[May 24, 2019] I attended the conference Approximation Theory 16 with Ryunosuke Oshiro, who gave a talk in this conference on May 20, 2019.

[Apr.15, 2019] A new preprint "Construction of conformal maps based on the locations of singularities for improving the double exponential formula", written in collaboration with Shunki Kyoya, has been released (arXiv:1904.05989).

[Jan.19, 2019] Our paper "Counting integral points in polytopes via numerical analysis of contour integration" (written with H. Hirai and R. Oshiro) has been accepted for publication in Mathematics of Operations Research.
